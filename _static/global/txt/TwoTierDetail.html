The Game:




Each order takes some time to arrive at the upper-tier:
  • The customers' order arrives at the retailer 2 periods after it has been placed.
  • The retailer's order arrives at the factory {{LTR2F}} periods after it has been placed.

    • Each delivery takes some time to arrive at the lower-tier:
  • The factory's delivery arrives at the retailer {{LTF2R}} periods after it has been sent.
  • The retailer's delivery arrives at the customer 2 periods after it has been sent.

    • The production also takes some time:
    The factory's order needs {{LTProd}} to be produced and arrive in its inventory.
    This time difference is called lead time.
