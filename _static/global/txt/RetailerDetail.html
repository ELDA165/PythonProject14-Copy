The Game:




You are the {{participant.role}}.


  • Each round you receive a customer order, which you need to fulfill. This order is fulfilled with the products in your inventory.
  • The customers order is constant at {{custDem}} each period.
  • If you cannot fulfill the orders, then you will have unfulfilled orders (i.e., backorder), which need to be fulfilled in the next rounds.
  • Your initial backorder is {{initBackl}}.

  • Each round you receive a incoming shipment, based on what you ordered in the past. This shipment will be added to your inventory.
  • If not all products in your inventory are needed, then you will have products left in your inventory.
  • Your initial inventory is {{initInv}}.

  • Each delivered item results in a turnover of {{turnover}} per item.
  • For each backorder item you have {{costsBackl}} per period.
  • For each item in your inventory, you pay {{costsInv}} per period.


    • Your task is to place an order with the factory each period.
