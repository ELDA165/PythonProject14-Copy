After/before each round you can see what happend in the previous round.


On the left side you can see the incoming demand and the outgoing shipments. In other words, your fulfilled orders.
On the right side you can see your past order placed (sent order), and your received shipments, which is added to your inventory.
In the middle you can see your inventory, after the incoming shipment is added and the outgoing shipments are dedacted.
You can also see how many orders you have not fulfilled over the past rounds.
